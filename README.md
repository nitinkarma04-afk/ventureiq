# 🚀 VyaparAI

AI-Powered Business Recommendation & Market Analysis Platform

## 📌 Problem Statement

Many aspiring entrepreneurs struggle to decide which business to start and where to establish it. Lack of market knowledge, budget planning, and risk analysis often leads to poor business decisions.

VyaparAI helps users make informed decisions by analyzing budget, location, risk factors, and business opportunities.

---

## ✨ Features

* Business Recommendation System
* Budget-Based Analysis
* Location-Based Suggestions
* Risk Analysis
* Profit Estimation
* Business Plan Saving
* AI Business Mentor
* Recommendation History
* Analytics Dashboard
